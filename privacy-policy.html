Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Scientific Calculator ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Core Calculator Functionality

The App's core calculator functions operate locally on your device. You do not need to create an account, register, or sign in to use the App.

Calculator expressions, results, matrices, equations, polynomial coefficients, calculus expressions, graph settings, and other mathematical inputs are processed locally on your device.

The developer does not operate a server that receives or stores your calculator expressions, calculation results, matrices, equations, graphs, or other mathematical content entered into the App.

The core calculator features can be used without an internet connection. However, internet access may be used by Google AdMob and Google's consent-management services for advertising and privacy-related functions.

Information the App Does Not Directly Collect

The App itself does not request, collect, or store personal information such as:

  • Your name
  • Your email address through the App
  • Your phone number
  • Your contacts
  • Your messages
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Photos, videos, or audio
  • Financial information
  • Health information
  • Passwords or authentication information
  • Precise device location

The App does not read or upload your existing personal files. When you choose to save a graph, the App creates a graph image and stores it locally on your device. The graph is not uploaded to a developer-controlled server.

Internet Access

The App's mathematical and calculator functions are designed to work locally. Internet access is used for Google AdMob advertisements and Google's privacy and consent-management services.

When the device is offline, advertisements and consent services may be unavailable, but the core calculator functions remain usable.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

The Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and share certain information in accordance with Google's policies and the user's applicable privacy choices. This information may include:

  • IP address, which may be used to estimate approximate location
  • Advertising ID
  • App Set ID
  • Other applicable device or account identifiers
  • Device and application information
  • App launches, taps, ad interactions, and other product-interaction information
  • Diagnostic and performance information
  • App launch time, hang rate, and energy-usage information

Google may process this information for purposes including:

  • Delivering advertisements
  • Advertising and marketing
  • Advertisement personalization, where permitted
  • Analytics and performance measurement
  • Fraud prevention, security, and abuse prevention
  • Improving Google's advertising services

Depending on your region, consent choices, device settings, and Google's advertising configuration, the App may display personalized, non-personalized, or limited advertisements.

The developer may receive aggregated advertising reports through Google AdMob. The developer does not provide your calculator expressions or mathematical inputs to Google AdMob for advertising purposes.

Consent and Privacy Choices

The App uses Google's User Messaging Platform where applicable to request and manage advertising privacy choices.

When required by applicable law or Google's services, the App may display a consent message before advertisements are requested.

A Privacy Options control may also be available inside the App when Google's User Messaging Platform determines that users must be able to review or modify their privacy choices.

Users may also reset or delete their Android advertising ID through the privacy or advertising settings available on their Android device.

Data Sharing

The developer does not sell, rent, or share your calculator expressions, results, matrices, equations, polynomial inputs, calculus inputs, or graph settings.

Advertising-related information may be collected or shared by Google through the Google Mobile Ads SDK, as described in this Privacy Policy and Google's documentation.

The App does not currently include:

  • A developer-operated user-account system
  • A developer-operated cloud synchronization service
  • A developer-controlled analytics service
  • A developer-controlled crash-reporting service
  • A developer-operated advertising server

Data Retention and Deletion

The developer does not receive or maintain a server-side copy of your calculator expressions, results, matrices, equations, polynomial inputs, calculus inputs, graph settings, or saved graph images.

Because the App does not provide user accounts or store calculator content on a developer-controlled server, there is no developer-held calculator account or calculator dataset requiring an account-deletion request.

Locally stored information can be removed by:

  • Clearing the relevant information within the App
  • Deleting exported graph images from your device
  • Clearing the App's storage through Android settings
  • Uninstalling the App

Advertising-related information processed by Google is retained and deleted according to Google's policies, applicable laws, device settings, and user privacy choices.

Device Backup

Android backup and device-transfer backup are disabled for the App's private application data.

Files that you deliberately save outside the App's private storage, such as exported graph images, remain subject to your device's storage, synchronization, and backup settings.

Security

The App is designed to minimize data handling by processing mathematical content locally.

According to Google's Mobile Ads SDK disclosure, advertising-related data handled by the Google Mobile Ads SDK is encrypted in transit using Transport Layer Security.

Reasonable measures are taken to protect the integrity of the App. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically designed or directed toward children under the age of 13. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

The App's target-audience selection and advertising configuration will be maintained in accordance with applicable Google Play and Google advertising requirements.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child has provided personal information directly to the developer, please contact the developer using the email address below.
